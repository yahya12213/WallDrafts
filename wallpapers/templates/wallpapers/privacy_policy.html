Privacy Policy

No registration required. Minimal data collection for anonymous browsing.

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Key Privacy Feature: No Registration Required

WallDrafts is designed to respect your privacy from the ground up. We don't require user registration, meaning we don't collect personal information like names, emails, or passwords.

This Privacy Policy explains how WallDrafts ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our wallpaper download service. Since no registration is required, we collect minimal data necessary to provide and improve our service.

1. Information We Collect

What We DON'T Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, address)
  • No account creation or login data
  • No payment information (service is completely free)
  • No social media login data

What We DO Collect

We collect only the minimal data necessary for service operation:

1.1 Automatic Server Logs

Like most websites, our servers automatically record basic information:

Data Type Purpose Retention Anonymized
IP Address Security, analytics, fraud prevention 30 days Yes - Hashed
Browser Type Compatibility optimization 90 days Yes
Device Information Responsive design optimization 90 days Yes
Access Timestamp Analytics and usage patterns 1 year Yes

1.2 Download Analytics

When you download a wallpaper, we collect anonymous analytics:

  • Wallpaper ID (to track popularity)
  • Timestamp of download
  • Device type (desktop/mobile)
  • Hashed IP address (for security, not identification)
  • User agent (browser/device info, truncated to 500 chars)

1.3 Session Data (Browser Storage)

Features like "likes" and "favorites" are stored locally in your browser:

  • Stored in your browser's session storage
  • Not transmitted to our servers
  • Cleared when you close your browser
  • Specific to your device and browser

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Improvement Only

We use collected data exclusively to improve our service:

  • Service Operation: To provide and maintain wallpaper downloads
  • Analytics: To understand popular wallpapers and improve content
  • Security: To detect and prevent abuse or fraudulent activity
  • Technical Optimization: To ensure compatibility across devices
  • Content Curation: To identify trending wallpapers

We never sell, rent, or trade your data. We don't use data for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the minimal data we collect:

  • IP addresses are hashed (SHA-256) before storage
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Limited data retention periods
  • Regular security audits

4. Cookies and Tracking

Minimal Cookie Usage

We use only essential cookies for basic functionality:

  • Session Cookies: Required for features like favorites and likes
  • No Tracking Cookies: We don't use cookies for advertising or tracking
  • No Third-Party Cookies: Only first-party cookies from our domain
  • Easy Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings

5. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Web Hosting: Standard web hosting services
  • Analytics: Basic server-side analytics (not Google Analytics)
  • No Advertising Networks: We don't use advertising services
  • No Social Media Plugins: No Facebook, Twitter, or other social widgets

6. Your Privacy Rights

Since we collect minimal data and don't have user accounts, your rights are straightforward:

Your Control Options

  • Browser Controls: Clear cookies/session data anytime via browser settings
  • Private Browsing: Use incognito/private mode for completely anonymous browsing
  • No Account Deletion: Since no account exists, nothing to delete
  • Opt-Out: You can choose not to download wallpapers

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for all ages. We don't collect age information, but we ensure our content is appropriate for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will update the "Last Updated" date when changes are made.
